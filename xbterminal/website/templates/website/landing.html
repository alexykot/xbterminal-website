Merchants FAQ

Why would I want to accept Bitcoin payments?

There are several advantages to using bitcoin over regular credit or debit cards. The first is that fees are substantially lower than any existing card system. The second main advantage is that there is no chargeback mechanism so once you have received customer’s coins they can’t be taken from you via chargeback.

How secure is this payment system?

Chargebacks and payment card fraud are not applicable to Bitcoin, so this system is much more secure and existing fraud percent is virtually none.
It works like digital cash - once you have received payment nobody can take it away from you.

What does "NFC enabled" mean?

NFC stands for Near Field Communication. It is a two way radio communication designed to work at very close range, only within few inches, i.e. "on touch".
This is a system very similar to contactless cards (but not compatible). Currently many android phones are equipped with NFC.

Will you protect me from bitcoin unpredictable exchange rate?

Yes. Our terminal is integrated with Bitcoin payment processors, which will instantly convert bitcoins paid to you into your local currency and send it to your bank account at the end of the day.
Available payment processors: BitPay, Cryptopay, BIPS.
We will help you to choose and sign up with the most suitable payment processor considering your geographical location.

So how much does it cost and when can I get it?

Pre­order units will cost £200 GBP. Delivery will be on a first come first served basis, with one month lead time after crowdfunding campaign ending.
