What is FIAT money?

FIAT money is basically any currency recognised by a government as legal tender. In our context we usually mean your local currency, like GBP or USD.

Payment processing

If your business needs FIAT currency rather than holding bitcoin, we integrate with several payment processors. Currently we integrate with BIPS, Bitpay and Cryptopay.

Payment Security

Bitcoin is a very secure protocol. We do not handle your coins or FIAT, your XBTerminal handles the whole process to either transfer funds to your business wallet or directly to the payment processor.

Why Bitcoin over card payments?

There are several advantages to using bitcoin over regular credit or debit cards. The first is that fees are substantially lower than any existing card system. The second main advantage is that there is no chargeback mechanism so once you have received customer’s coins they can’t be taken from you via chargeback.
